Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Agora Blog ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. This is a portfolio project created for demonstration purposes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: First name, last name (optional)
  • Content: Blog posts, comments, and other user-generated content
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and access logs
  • Authentication Data: Two-factor authentication settings and TOTP secrets (encrypted)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the blog platform
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To enable social features (following, profiles, etc.)
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To improve our services and user experience

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using strong cryptographic algorithms
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available for additional security
  • CAPTCHA protection against automated attacks
  • Secure session management with HttpOnly and Secure cookies
  • OWASP security headers (HSTS, X-Frame-Options, CSP, etc.)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. As this is a portfolio project, data is stored solely for demonstration purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information through your profile settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of optional features

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. These cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

This platform is hosted on Heroku and uses the following third-party services:

  • Heroku: Cloud hosting platform (see Heroku's Privacy Policy)
  • PostgreSQL: Database storage

9. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Portfolio Project Notice

Important: This is a portfolio project created for demonstration purposes. While we implement industry-standard security practices, this platform should not be used for storing sensitive or confidential information.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the platform's contact methods.
